I am really not great about updating my site. It is something that I have really wanted to get better at doing over, let’s say...the last year. There are a lot of reasons why everything stops and starts, but I don't need to get into those. This is something that I am really working on. I want to be more "present" on my own site and not let it just sit here inactive. Do I want to be some kind of thought leader/big voice in the web world? No, not really. What I want is to just have a voice and show what my mind is thinking, but I am distracted and burnt out.

I took off two weeks of December for the holidays and thought about what I want from my website and some goal setting! In 2026, I am going to really work on writing more. Sharing who I am, what I do, and what I like. Tell people about it and what new skills I am working on and learning. This is a very new and very interesting challenge for myself. I am really interested in taking it on and making a go at it. Maybe it becomes something more. Maybe it doesn’t, but I do know I am pushing myself forward by at least practicing what I preach (at least a little bit).

Another part of this process is slowing down and being more intentional with my process and work. I know that if I sit down and physically write out what I am going to do. Keep everything very simple and slow. Reducing my digital clutter as well is part of this (partly by practicing what I preach on this as well). I know this is a long road, and I am (re)taking my steps on it. 

This new year, I will work on sharing my passions outside the web as well. My journey of hiking in the White Mountains and my home state, Vermont. Maybe discuss more about my fitness and sport passions. Most of all, I am going to talk about and write about what excites me.

That will push me forward more than anything else. Having fun.
